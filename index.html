Courtship

First Meet

Wednesday, July 1st ,2020

Lily and Andrew first met at a garden party hosted by a mutual friend. Lily, always organized, had arrived early to help set up. Andrew, on the other hand, showed up late with a casual excuse about getting lost in a park. Despite their differences, they hit it off instantly—Andrew’s easygoing humor made Lily laugh, while her calm, thoughtful nature intrigued him. That first meeting sparked a connection neither of them expected.

Fisrt Date

Thursday, October 20th ,2022

A few months after their first meeting, Andrew invited Lily out for coffee at a cozy local café. It wasn’t anything fancy, but it was perfect. They spent hours talking, laughing, and discovering how much they enjoyed each other’s company. The simplicity of the evening made it unforgettable, as they both realized that love doesn’t need grand gestures—it grows in quiet, shared moments.

Marriage Proposal

Wednesday, August 21st ,2024

On a quiet hike to one of their favorite spots, Andrew surprised Lily with a proposal. As they stood overlooking a beautiful valley, he got down on one knee, ring in hand, and asked her to marry him. It was spontaneous, sincere, and perfectly them. With tears in her eyes, Lily said yes, knowing that their future together would be full of love and adventure
